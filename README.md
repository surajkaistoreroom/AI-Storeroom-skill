# AI Storeroom — Claude Skill

**Open Source Claude Skill: AI Product Advisor**

Builds custom AI stacks for companies using a curated catalog of the best AI products and agents.

![Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/800x200?text=AI+Storeroom) <!-- Replace with real banner later -->

## ✨ What it does
Give it your industry, company size, goals, and constraints — it delivers:
- Tailored AI product recommendations
- Full stack architecture
- Realistic ROI estimates
- Setup guides + risks
- Conservative business impact analysis

Perfect for CTOs, Heads of AI, Product Leaders, and consultants.

## 🚀 Quick Start
1. Install this skill in Claude (via SKILL.md)
2. Ask:  
   `"Build an AI stack for a Series A B2B SaaS company focused on customer support and sales"`
